Our Story · Est. 2024

Two homes, one people.

Marshallese families live all across Arizona, from Tucson up to Flagstaff, though most of us settle in the Phoenix area. People come here for work, for school, and for medical care they can't get back home. AMC exists to close that gap - one family, one question, one connection at a time.

We started AMC in 2024 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, run by Marshallese for Marshallese. We help families get set up, whether that is helping with I-94 update, enrolling onto health insurance, or applying for scholarships, we keep our culture front and center while we do it.

When one of us learns the way, we mark it for the next canoe. That's the whole idea.
